Transportation Couldn't Get Any Easier!

Here in Park Cty, we have several options to help you get from point A to B. We even have transportation on the mountains! Here is some helpful information for you:

  • Free Bus System: Runs from 5:30am til 11pm. Here for schedule changes.
  • Uber/Lyft: Both of these companies have many employees driving thru our city. You should never has an issue finding a ride.
  • Shuttles: We have shuttles that travel from Park City to places like: Salt Lake City, All Resorts, and More.
  • Lifts: 2 Lifts on PCMR. One to Canyons and the other to Downtown PC.
